Tribe Guidelines & FAQ

Core Rules

Check-in Info

Starts at 4:00 PM. Entry will not be permitted without a valid entry pass.

Liquor Policy

BYOB Allowed. Only Telangana State Liquor permitted. No sales at venue. No foreign/defence liquor.

Strictly Prohibited

Drugs and Hookah are strictly banned on premises. Zero tolerance policy.

Common Questions

Is parking available?
Yes, we have a designated parking area for both 2-wheelers and 4-wheelers.
Are washrooms available?
Yes, we have clean, separate washrooms for men and women maintained regularly.
Is it safe for families/couples?
Absolutely. We are a family-friendly eco-resort with security on site to ensure a safe environment for everyone.
Can we bring our own food?
Outside food is generally not encouraged as we provide a full buffet. However, small snacks are allowed.
